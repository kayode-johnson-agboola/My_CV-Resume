Presently, my research focus is in the investigation of Ultra-low velocity zones which are anomalous structure at the core-mantle boundary region of the Earth.

Master's Thesis Research 2021-Present

Thesis Title: Ultra-low Velocity Zone Assessment Beneath the Southern Hemisphere: An Application of Historical Interstation Pattern Referencing Technique and Double Array Stacking of PcP Waveforms.
  • Acquiring Seismic data from Incorporated Research Institutions for Seismology datalistManagement Management Center (IRIS DMC), Réseau Sismoloqique Français (RESIF), and GEOFONschungsNetz(GEOFON) international data repositories.

  • Modelling of waveforms and generation of synthetic seismogram to interpret structures.

Undergraduate Research Project 2018

Thesis Title: Petrophysical Parameter of Evaluation of Wells "X" and "Y", Niger Delta Oil Field
  • Used wireline log data (Gamma Ray(GR), Resistivity, Neutron, Density, and Spontaneous Potential (SP) logs) to delineate reservoir rocks.
  • Reservoir rock properties (e.g., porosity, permeability), and fluid contents (oil, gas or water) were evaluated.
  • Volume of fluids (hydrocarbon) in the reservoir rock was estimated.
  • Recommendation regarding reservoirs with high economic potential was given based on estimated values of porosity, permeability, and hydrocarbon saturation.
